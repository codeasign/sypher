The global e-learning market is projected to reach $1 trillion by 2028. Yet most platforms ship static courses — the same content for every student, regardless of their background, pace, or learning style. An AI-native learning platform can generate personalized curricula, adapt difficulty based on performance, and provide instant feedback on exercises.

## What You'll Build

You'll build **LearnAI**, an AI-powered learning platform with:

- **Course generation** — enter any topic, get a structured curriculum with lessons, quizzes, and exercises
- **Adaptive difficulty** — quizzes adjust difficulty based on performance
- **Progress tracking** — per-lesson and per-course completion metrics
- **Code exercises** — in-browser coding challenges with AI evaluation
- **Flashcard generation** — auto-generated flashcards from lesson content
- **Study recommendations** — AI suggests what to review based on weak areas

## Technology Decisions

| Component | Choice | Why this, not X |
|---|---|---|
| Course Generation | Claude Haiku | Fast, cheap — generates a full course under $0.05. Sonnet would be more creative but 5x cost |
| Quiz Evaluation | Claude Sonnet 4 | Evaluating open-ended answers needs deep reasoning. Haiku misses nuanced errors |
| Frontend | Jinja2 templates | Simpler to start. React would be better for interactive exercises later |
| Storage | SQLite | Sufficient for single-user or small team. PostgreSQL needed for multi-tenant |
